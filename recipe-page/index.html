Favabeans with Tomato (فول بالطماطم)

A simple and delicious recipe for a traditional Middle Eastern dish that combines the earthy flavors of fava beans with the freshness of tomatoes, perfect for a hearty meal or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of favabeans
  • 3 fresh medium-sized raw tomatoes
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp of vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 fresh lemon
  • Spices: salt, black paper, cumin, coriander

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Cut the onion and the tomatoes into small cubes, and mince the garlic.
  2. Step 2: Fry the onion in a pot until soft.
  3. Step 3: Add the minced garlic and stir for 1 minute.
  4. Step 4: Add the tomatoes and braise it on low heat.
  5. Step 5: Add the favabeans and the spices to the pot and leave for about 5-10 minutes with occasional stirring.
  6. Step 6: Squeeze the lemon over the favabeans and give it a last stir
  7. Step 7: Serve with hot baladi bread عيش بلدى.
